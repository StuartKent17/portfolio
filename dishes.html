Dish drying solution.

Problem:

Run off water damaging sink lining due to excess time drying.

Constraints*:

  1. Lazy; must be able to do dishes and leave to dry
  2. Bulk washing; Must be able to do a large number of dishes at once
  3. Compact; We have a small kitchen, must make use of space efficiently
  4. Cost effective; Must not be a large amount of money, it's a bloody dish rack
  5. Flexible; Must fit in to or enhance current process
  6. Fix Problem; Must stop water from getting on bench
  7. Improve Situation; Could run water into sink and reduce maintenance time
*Standard dish racks were not included as they do not meet the important 6th requirement.
**Prices and products are based what I could source locally, due to the short-time frame in which this was required. Mall Tax.
